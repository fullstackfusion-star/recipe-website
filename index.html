Classic Pancakes 🥞

Ingredients

  • 1 cup flour
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tbsp melted butter

Instructions

Mix dry ingredients in one bowl and wet ingredients in another. Combine them gently and cook on a buttered skillet until golden brown on both sides. Serve with syrup.

Spaghetti Aglio e Olio 🍝

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 3 cloves garlic (sliced)
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1/2 tsp chili flakes
  • Salt and parsley to taste

Instructions

Cook pasta until al dente. In a pan, heat olive oil and sauté garlic until golden. Add chili flakes, then toss in spaghetti. Garnish with parsley and serve hot.

Chocolate Chip Cookies 🍪

Ingredients

  • 1 cup flour
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup chocolate chips
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda

Instructions

Cream butter and sugar together, add egg, then mix in dry ingredients. Stir in chocolate chips. Bake at 180°C (350°F) for 10–12 minutes until golden brown.
